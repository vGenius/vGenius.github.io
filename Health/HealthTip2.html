Top 10 Foods Highest in Vitamin E

Vitamin E is a collective name of eight fat-soluble vitamins with distinct antioxidant properties. The naturally occurring vitamin E exists in eight chemical forms- alpha-, beta-, gamma-, and delta-tocopherol and alpha-, beta-, gamma-, and delta-tocotrienol. Alpha tocopherol is the only form of vitamin E that is recognized to meet the human requirements.
